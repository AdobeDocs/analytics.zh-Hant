>[!BEGINSHADEBOX]

*本文記錄了&#x200B;**Adobe Analytics**中的環形圖視覺效果。<br/>檢視此文章的&#x200B;**Customer Journey Analytics**版本的[環形圖](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/en/docs/analytics-platform/using/cja-workspace/visualizations/donut)。*
